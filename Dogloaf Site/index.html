Lasting Wisdom from My Dad

'Hey dad, what's the weather supposed to be like today?'
'Eh, it might rain. Or it might not'.
Well, yes. Those are the only two possible outcomes. Like those who learned at the feet of Marcus Aurelius, I will remember this stoic truth-bomb for the rest of my life.
You have no control over the weather, but what you do have control over is your attitude.
Be ready.
